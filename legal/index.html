Legal & Platform Policies

Tailors.lk operates as a digital marketplace connecting customers, tailors, brands and partners across Sri Lanka.

This page explains our legal framework, responsibilities, limitations and platform rules governing the use of Tailors.lk as Sri Lanka's premier tailoring marketplace.

View Terms Contact Legal Team

Platform Introduction

Tailors.lk is a technology and discovery platform that connects customers with professional tailors, brands, and service providers across Sri Lanka. Our platform operates as a digital marketplace facilitating connections, communication, and service discovery.

We provide digital infrastructure for listings, lead routing, communication tools, and platform features that enhance the tailoring experience. Our role is to facilitate connections while maintaining a secure, reliable platform for all users.

Important: Tailors.lk does not directly stitch, manufacture, or deliver tailoring services unless explicitly stated in specific partnership arrangements. We are a discovery and connection platform.

Platform Role & Disclaimer

Understanding our platform role is essential for all users:

  • Tailors.lk is a Discovery Platform: We connect customers with independent tailors and brands but do not provide tailoring services directly.
  • Service Provider Independence: All tailoring services are executed by independent tailors, brands, or service providers listed on our platform.
  • Quality Responsibility: Service quality, pricing, delivery timelines, and workmanship belong to the service provider, not Tailors.lk.
  • Communication Facilitation: We provide tools for communication but are not party to agreements between users and service providers.
  • Promotion Only: Our platform provides discovery, communication facilitation, and promotional services for the tailoring community.

By using Tailors.lk, you acknowledge and accept that we are a platform connecting independent service providers with customers.

User Responsibilities

For Customers

  • Provide accurate measurements and specifications for tailoring work
  • Communicate clearly and professionally with service providers
  • Review service agreements, pricing, and terms before making payments
  • Respect tailor policies, timelines, and professional requirements
  • Provide constructive feedback and maintain professional relationships
  • Report any concerns or issues through proper platform channels

For Tailors & Brands

  • Maintain accurate, current, and honest business listings
  • Follow all applicable Sri Lankan laws and regulations
  • Deliver quality services as promised in listings and agreements
  • Keep profile information, pricing, and availability updated regularly
  • Communicate professionally and respond to inquiries promptly
  • Respect customer privacy and protect personal information
  • Adhere to industry standards and best practices

Listings, Verification & Featured Status

Listing Approval & Verification

  • Registration Does Not Guarantee Approval: Submitting a registration does not automatically result in platform listing
  • Verification Process: Tailors.lk reviews and verifies all listings before activation
  • Quality Standards: Listings must meet our quality, compliance, and professional standards
  • Suspension & Removal: We reserve the right to suspend or remove listings that violate policies or quality standards

Featured Status & Promotion

  • Featured Placements: May involve subscription models, promotional campaigns, or tender opportunities
  • Verification Factors: Includes compliance history, quality ratings, performance metrics, and user feedback
  • Visibility Control: Tailors.lk controls promotional visibility and placement algorithms
  • Performance Monitoring: Featured status may be adjusted based on performance and compliance

Note: Featured placements are promotional opportunities and do not imply endorsement or guarantee of service quality. Customers should exercise their own judgment when selecting service providers.

Commercial Structures

  • Evolution of Structures: Commercial terms may evolve as the platform grows and adapts to market needs

Understanding our liability limitations is essential for platform use:

  • Service Execution: Tailors.lk is not liable for service execution, quality, or delivery by listed providers
  • User Interactions: We are not responsible for losses, delays, or damages resulting from user interactions
  • Platform Availability: The platform is provided "as is" without warranties of uninterrupted access
  • Service Provider Risk: Users engage service providers at their own risk and discretion
  • Information Accuracy: While we verify information, we cannot guarantee absolute accuracy of all listings
  • Third-Party Services: Not responsible for external services, websites, or tools linked from our platform

Important: Users should conduct their own due diligence when selecting and engaging with service providers on our platform. Tailors.lk facilitates connections but does not guarantee outcomes.

Account Termination & Access Control

We maintain platform integrity through access control measures:

Termination Grounds

  • Policy Violations: Accounts may be suspended for policy violations or abusive behavior
  • Fraudulent Activity: Any fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading practices may result in termination
  • Quality Breaches: Consistent quality issues or customer complaints may lead to access removal
  • Spam & Misrepresentation: Spamming users or misrepresenting services is prohibited
  • Legal Compliance: Violation of applicable laws or regulations may result in account suspension

Platform Authority

  • Discretionary Control: Tailors.lk may remove access without obligation or prior notice
  • Appeal Process: Suspended accounts may request review through proper channels
  • Content Removal: We reserve the right to remove content that violates policies or standards
  • System Monitoring: Platform usage may be monitored for compliance and security purposes

Governing Law

All platform usage and legal matters are governed by:

  • Applicable Law: Governed by the laws of Sri Lanka without regard to conflict of law principles
  • Jurisdiction: Legal matters fall under the jurisdiction of courts within Sri Lanka
  • Compliance: Platform operations comply with Sri Lankan regulations and requirements
  • International Users: International users agree to Sri Lankan jurisdiction for platform-related matters

By using Tailors.lk, you submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of Sri Lankan courts for any disputes arising from platform use.

Tailors.lk: Sri Lanka's Premier Tailoring Platform

Tailors.lk serves as Sri Lanka's leading digital marketplace for tailoring services, connecting customers with professional tailors across the island. Our platform revolutionizes how Sri Lankans discover, connect with, and engage tailoring professionals for all their clothing needs.

As a comprehensive tailoring platform in Sri Lanka, we support both traditional craftsmanship and modern tailoring techniques. From custom suits in Colombo to boutique tailoring services in Kandy and Galle, our marketplace brings together the best of Sri Lanka's tailoring talent in one accessible digital space.

Our online tailoring services in Sri Lanka cater to diverse needs including bespoke tailoring, alterations, custom designs, and specialized clothing services. Whether you're looking for traditional Sri Lankan attire, contemporary fashion, or professional business wear, our platform connects you with verified tailors who understand both local styles and international standards.

The tailor marketplace in Colombo and throughout Sri Lanka benefits from our digital infrastructure that enhances discovery, communication, and service coordination. We're committed to supporting Sri Lanka's tailoring industry while making professional services more accessible to customers nationwide through our innovative platform approach.

Tailors.lk represents the future of tailoring services in Sri Lanka - combining traditional craftsmanship with modern digital convenience. Our platform supports the growth of Sri Lanka's tailoring industry while ensuring customers can easily find and connect with professional service providers for all their tailoring needs.
