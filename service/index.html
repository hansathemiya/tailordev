Frequently Asked Questions About Tailoring Services

How do I provide measurements for custom tailoring?

You can provide measurements in three ways: 1) Enter measurements directly on our platform using our measurement guide, 2) Visit the tailor in person for professional measuring, or 3) Use your existing well-fitting clothing as reference. Most tailors also offer guidance on taking accurate measurements at home.

What is the typical turnaround time for tailoring services?

Turnaround time varies by service: Simple alterations take 1-3 days, custom shirts and blouses 3-7 days, suits and formal wear 7-14 days, wedding outfits 2-4 weeks, and bulk orders 2-6 weeks depending on quantity. Each tailor's profile shows their estimated timelines for different services.

Can I see examples of a tailor's work before ordering?

Yes! Every tailor on Tailors.lk has a portfolio section showcasing their previous work. You can browse photos of completed projects, read customer reviews, and see ratings before making your decision. This helps you choose a tailor whose style and quality match your expectations.

What if I need alterations after receiving my custom clothing?

Most tailors offer free minor adjustments within 7-14 days of delivery if the garment doesn't fit perfectly. For significant alterations needed due to measurement errors, there may be additional charges. Always communicate clearly with your tailor about fit expectations and review policies before ordering.
