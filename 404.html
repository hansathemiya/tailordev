Tailors.lk Platform Development

Tailors.lk is continuously expanding its platform to serve Sri Lanka's tailoring community better. We're developing new marketplaces, tools, and programs that will transform how tailors, customers, and businesses connect across the island.

From fabric sourcing and export opportunities to advanced automation tools and professional training, our upcoming features are designed to elevate Sri Lanka's tailoring industry. Each new program is built with input from tailors, customers, and industry experts across the country.

Whether you're a tailor looking to expand your business, a customer seeking premium services, or a business needing bulk tailoring solutions, Tailors.lk is creating the ecosystem to support Sri Lanka's fashion and garment industry growth.
