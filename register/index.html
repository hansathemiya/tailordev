Frequently Asked Questions

Is registration on Tailors.lk free?

Yes, registration on Tailors.lk is completely free. There are no registration fees or hidden costs. You only pay a small commission when you receive and complete orders through our platform.

How long does the registration process take?

After submitting your registration form, our team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days. Once approved, you can set up your profile and start receiving customer inquiries immediately.

What information do I need to provide?

You need to provide your basic contact information, business details, location, and the services you offer. You'll also need to create a profile with photos of your work once your registration is approved.

How do I get paid for orders?

Customers pay through our secure platform. After you complete an order and the customer confirms satisfaction, payment is released to you minus our platform commission. You can choose to receive payments via bank transfer or other supported methods.

Can I register if I work from home?

Absolutely! Many tailors on our platform work from home. You just need to provide your location so customers in your area can find you. We support both home-based tailors and those with physical shops.
