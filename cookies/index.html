Cookies Policy – Tailors.lk

This Cookies Policy explains how Tailors.lk uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience, analytics, security, and platform functionality. This policy applies to all visitors, customers, tailors, partners, franchisees, and learners using our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and improve user experience, as well as to provide information to website owners.

At Tailors.lk, we use cookies to remember your preferences, maintain your session, understand how you interact with our platform, and improve our services for the Sri Lankan tailoring community.

Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device. They are simply text files that help websites recognize your device.

2. Why Tailors.lk Uses Cookies

We use cookies for several essential purposes to ensure our platform functions properly and provides the best experience for users across Sri Lanka:

  • Website Functionality: To enable basic features like page navigation, search functionality, and access to secure areas.
  • Search Experience: To remember your search preferences and location settings when looking for tailors in Colombo, Kandy, Galle, or other Sri Lankan cities.
  • Session Handling: To keep you signed in to your account and maintain your preferences throughout your visit.
  • Security: To protect against fraudulent activity and verify your identity when accessing your account.
  • Analytics and Performance: To understand how visitors use our platform, which pages are most popular, and how we can improve our services.
  • Platform Optimization: To ensure our website loads quickly and functions properly across different devices and internet connections in Sri Lanka.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, form submissions, and search functionality. Without these cookies, Tailors.lk would not be able to provide its core services.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices (such as your language preference, location settings, or login information) to improve your experience on subsequent visits.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our platform. These cookies help us track page views, search behavior, and usage patterns to improve our services for the Sri Lankan tailoring community.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us optimize our platform's performance by collecting information about how visitors use our website. This helps us identify and fix errors, improve load times, and enhance overall user experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services that provide analytics, hosting, or embedded tools. These may include:

  • Analytics providers
  • Embedded maps and location services
  • Social media integrations
  • Payment processing services
Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your login session and preferences Session only
Preference Cookies Remember your location, language, and display preferences Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Track website usage and improve services Up to 2 years
Security Cookies Protect against fraudulent activity Session only

4. Third-Party Technologies

Tailors.lk may use services from third-party providers that place their own cookies on your device. These services may include:

  • Analytics Services: To understand how our platform is used and improve our services
  • Hosting Providers: To ensure reliable website performance across Sri Lanka
  • Embedded Services: Such as maps, videos, or social media features
  • External Integrations: For payment processing or communication tools

Please note that Tailors.lk does not control the cookies placed by these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy and cookie policies for more information.

Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. Tailors.lk is not responsible for the cookie practices of external services.

5. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are essential for maintaining your session and preferences during a single visit to Tailors.lk.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period (from days to years) unless you delete them manually. They remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

Cookie Lifespan

The duration of cookies varies depending on their purpose:

  • Session cookies: Expire when you close your browser
  • Preference cookies: Typically last up to 1 year
  • Analytics cookies: Usually last between 30 days to 2 years
  • Security cookies: Generally session-based or short-term

6. How You Can Control Cookies

You have several options to control how cookies are used on your device when visiting Tailors.lk:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Manual Deletion

You can manually delete cookies from your browser at any time through your browser settings. This will remove all cookies stored by websites you've visited.

Opt-Out Tools

For analytics and advertising cookies, you may be able to use opt-out tools provided by third-party services.

Important: If you choose to block or delete cookies, some features of Tailors.lk may not function properly. Essential services like searching for tailors, contacting professionals, or accessing your account may be affected.

7. Consent

By using Tailors.lk, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. This includes cookies placed by both Tailors.lk and our trusted third-party partners.

We may implement additional cookie consent mechanisms in the future, such as cookie banners or preference centers, to give you more granular control over cookie settings.

If you do not agree to our use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings as described in Section 6 or refrain from using our platform.

8. Changes to This Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or services. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.

We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we use cookies and similar technologies. Continued use of Tailors.lk after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes will be communicated through our platform or via email to registered users when appropriate.
