Customer Requests & Orders

How customer inquiries reach you through Tailors.lk

Tailors.lk receives inquiries from customers looking for tailoring services across Sri Lanka. We match customer needs with suitable tailors based on services offered, location, and availability.

Customer requests are routed to tailors who have indicated they provide the specific service needed. Higher membership plans receive more suitable customer opportunities and priority routing.

You maintain full control over which jobs to accept based on your capacity and expertise. Each inquiry includes customer details and project requirements for your consideration.
