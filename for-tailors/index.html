How to Join as a Tailor

Simple registration process designed for Sri Lankan tailors. Start growing your business in just a few steps.

1

Register Your Business

Visit register.tailors.lk and submit your basic business information. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

2

Verification & Contact

Our team reviews your submission and contacts you via WhatsApp or phone to confirm details and understand your specialization.

3

Complete Your Profile

You receive an advanced profile form to collect services, pricing ranges, portfolio images, and detailed business information.

4

Profile Publication

Your tailor profile is created and published on Tailors.lk, making you visible to customers searching for your services.

5

Become Verified

After meeting quality and service standards, you are promoted to Verified Tailor status for increased trust and visibility.
