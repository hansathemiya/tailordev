Frequently Asked Questions About Tailoring Services

How do I provide measurements for custom tailoring?

You can provide measurements in three ways: 1) Enter measurements online through our platform, 2) Visit the tailor directly for measurements, or 3) Request measurement guidance from the tailor. Many tailors provide detailed guides on how to take accurate measurements at home.

How long does tailoring usually take?

Tailoring time depends on the complexity of the garment and the tailor's workload. Simple alterations can take 1-2 days, custom shirts 3-5 days, suits 1-2 weeks, and wedding dresses 4-6 weeks. Each tailor shows their estimated turnaround time on their profile.

What if I'm not satisfied with the tailoring work?

Contact the tailor first to discuss any issues. Most tailors offer adjustments to ensure satisfaction. If unresolved, you can contact Tailors.lk support for mediation. Our platform holds payment until you confirm order completion, ensuring quality assurance.

Can I see examples of a tailor's previous work?

Yes, every tailor profile on Tailors.lk includes a portfolio section where they showcase their previous work. You can browse photos of completed garments to assess their style, quality, and attention to detail before making a decision.
