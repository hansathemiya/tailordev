Frequently Asked Questions About Tailoring Services

How do I find tailors near me in Sri Lanka?

Use Tailors.lk's location search feature to find tailors in your area. Simply enter your city or use "tailors near me" search to find professional tailors in Colombo, Kandy, Galle, and other Sri Lankan cities. You can filter by service type, ratings, and distance.

What should I look for in a good tailor in Colombo?

When looking for tailors in Colombo Sri Lanka, consider their: portfolio of previous work, customer reviews, specialization (suits, traditional wear, alterations), turnaround time, pricing transparency, and communication skills. Colombo tailors should have experience with both local and international fashion trends.

How much do tailoring services cost in Sri Lanka?

Tailoring costs vary based on service type, fabric quality, and tailor expertise. Simple alterations start from LKR 500, custom shirts from LKR 3,000, suits from LKR 15,000, and wedding dresses from LKR 25,000. Colombo tailors may charge slightly more due to higher operational costs and expertise.

Can I get same-day tailoring services?

Yes, many tailors offer express services for simple alterations and repairs. Look for tailors advertising "same-day service" or "express tailoring" on their profiles. For complex garments like suits or wedding dresses, allow 1-2 weeks minimum for quality work.
