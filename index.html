Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Tailors.lk to find professional tailoring services in Sri Lanka.

How can I contact a tailor if I have a problem?
Each tailor's profile on Tailors.lk includes their direct WhatsApp number, phone number, or social media links. You can also click the "Contact" button to message them via WhatsApp. For urgent issues, look for the "Emergency Service" filter to find tailors who can respond quickly.
What services are typically offered by tailors on Tailors.lk?
Tailors on our platform offer a wide range of professional services including:
  • Saree fitting and draping
  • Bespoke suit tailoring
  • Alterations for dresses, shirts, and formal wear
  • Hemming, button sewing, and fabric repairs
  • Custom design and embroidery
  • Emergency alterations for events or travel
You can browse each tailor's profile to see their specific services and reviews from real customers.
How do I find a trusted tailor near me on Tailors.lk?
Use our powerful search panel on the homepage! Enter your city (e.g., Colombo, Kandy, Galle) or a specific area, then select your service (like saree fitting or alterations). You can also filter by experience level, price range, or rating to find the perfect tailor. Our platform verifies and features top-rated tailors for your convenience.
How are tailors verified on Tailors.lk?
We ensure quality by verifying every tailor through a multi-step process:
  1. Profile Submission: Tailors submit their business details, photos, and service list.
  2. Document Check: We verify licenses, business registration, or professional certifications.
  3. Customer Reviews: Only tailors with real, verified reviews can appear on our directory.
  4. Featured Badge: Top-rated tailors (4.5+ stars, 5+ reviews) receive a "Featured" badge for visibility.
You can trust that every tailor listed has been vetted for professionalism and quality.
Is Tailors.lk free for customers to use?
Absolutely! Finding and connecting with tailors is completely free for customers. We help you discover local professionals without any cost. Tailors, however, pay a small fee to join our directory and get featured listings, which helps us maintain a high-quality, verified platform for you.
Can I book an appointment or contact a tailor directly?
Yes! Each tailor's profile includes their WhatsApp number, phone number, and sometimes a direct link to their website or social media. You can also click the "Contact" button to message them directly via WhatsApp. For urgent needs, look for the "Emergency Service" filter to find tailors who can accommodate same-day or next-day appointments.
What makes Tailors.lk different from other tailoring directories?
Tailors.lk is built for Sri Lankan users, with a focus on local, verified professionals. We offer:
  • Real customer reviews and ratings
  • Advanced filters (experience, price, availability, rating)
  • Emergency service options
  • Verified and featured tailors for trust and quality
  • A seamless, mobile-friendly search experience
  • Free access for customers â€” no hidden fees or paywalls
