3. Tailor Listing Process

The listing process for tailors on our platform follows a structured verification system:

3.1 Registration Phase

  1. Initial Registration: Submission via register.tailors.lk or the Platform's registration forms
  2. Verification Contact: Manual verification via phone call or email within 2-7 business days
  3. Secondary Form Collection: Detailed business information collection through advanced forms
  4. Profile Creation: Development of your public-facing tailor profile on the Platform
  5. Qualification Review: Assessment of your skills, portfolio, and business credentials
  6. Listing Approval: Final approval and publication of your profile

3.2 Key Conditions

  • Verification Not Guaranteed: Tailors.lk reserves the right to reject any listing application without providing reasons
  • Quality Standards: All listed tailors must maintain minimum quality standards as defined by Tailors.lk
  • Verification Revocation: Verified status can be revoked at any time if quality, reliability, or communication standards are not maintained
  • Profile Updates: Tailors must keep their profile information current and accurate

For more information about joining as a tailor, visit our For Tailors page.

Verified Tailor status is granted to tailors who meet specific quality and reliability standards:

  • Quality Standards: Consistent delivery of high-quality tailoring work
  • Reliability Metrics: On-time delivery and professional conduct
  • Communication: Responsive and clear communication with customers
  • Audit Compliance: Willingness to undergo periodic quality audits

Verification Rights: Tailors.lk may audit Verified Tailors at any time. Verification status can be removed without prior notice for failure to maintain standards.

4.2 Featured Tailors

Featured Tailor status is a premium subscription tier with additional benefits:

  • Paid Subscription: Monthly or annual recurring payment required
  • Automatic Verification: All Featured Tailors are automatically granted Verified status
  • Priority Placement: Featured placement in search results and category pages
  • Private Order Access: Eligibility for exclusive, high-value orders managed directly by Tailors.lk
  • Enhanced Visibility: Prominent display across the Platform

Platform Control: Tailors.lk maintains full control over profile placement, visibility settings, and order routing algorithms. Featured status does not guarantee specific order volume or revenue.

5. Order Allocation System

The Platform uses a multi-factor order allocation system designed to match customers with appropriate tailors while optimizing Platform performance.

5.1 Allocation Factors

Orders may be distributed based on one or more of the following factors:

  • Response Speed: Historical response times to customer enquiries
  • Payment Tier: Subscription level (Featured Tailors receive priority)
  • Tender Ownership: Exclusive rights for specific order categories
  • Internal Ranking: Quality scores, customer ratings, and performance metrics
  • Geographic Relevance: Proximity to customer location
  • Specialization Match: Alignment with order requirements
  • Staff Discretion: Manual allocation by Tailors.lk operations team

5.2 Platform Rights

Important Disclaimer: Tailors.lk reserves the full and absolute right to:

  • Route orders to any tailor based on Platform algorithms

  • Adjust allocation algorithms without prior notice

No Order Guarantees: Tailors.lk does not guarantee any minimum order volume, frequency, or value to any tailor, regardless of subscription tier or performance history.
