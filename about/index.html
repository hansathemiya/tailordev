About Tailors.lk

Tailors.lk connects customers with professional tailors across Sri Lanka for custom suits, alterations, bridal wear, corporate uniforms, and casual or luxury tailoring. We are building Sri Lanka's national tailoring network—a complete ecosystem where quality meets accessibility.

The Story Behind Tailors.lk

Several years ago, I operated a small tailoring setup in Colombo. Walk‑in clients were steady, but growth was limited to the neighbourhood. I paused that business when I realised offline‑only tailoring could not scale to meet Sri Lanka's potential.

Later, I needed a custom suit for an important event. Despite Colombo having hundreds of tailors, I struggled to find one who was responsive, transparent about timelines, and could deliver quality on short notice. That missed opportunity exposed a clear problem: great tailors exist, but visibility and trust are broken.

The real turning point came during a conversation with an ambitious tailor in Kandy. He was skilled, motivated, and had a growing local reputation—but he had no budget for a website, no digital presence, and no way to reach customers outside his area. He told me, "If I could just be found online, I could double my business."

That became the trigger for Tailors.lk. We decided to build infrastructure for tailors, not just another directory. Our goal is to give every tailor in Sri Lanka the tools to grow, and every customer the confidence to order custom clothing.

The Problem We Solve

For Customers

Finding a verified, reliable tailor in Sri Lanka is often a matter of word‑of‑mouth or luck. Customers struggle with unclear pricing, uncertain timelines, and no easy way to compare quality or reviews.

For Tailors

Skilled tailors lack the digital tools to reach a wider audience. Marketing, online booking, and building trust with new customers are constant hurdles—especially for small workshops and independent artisans.

Tailors.lk bridges both sides with technology and operations—creating a trusted, efficient platform for Sri Lanka's tailoring community.
