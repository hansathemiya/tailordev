Platform Policies & Service Rules

These policies define how orders, services, refunds, cancellations, quality standards and platform usage work on Tailors.lk across Sri Lanka. Understanding these policies ensures smooth transactions and clear expectations for all platform users.

Order Classification Policy

All orders on Tailors.lk fall into one of three categories, each with different responsibilities and processes. Understanding these categories is essential for both customers and tailors.

Platform-Managed Orders (Tailors.lk Responsibility)

  • Direct Platform Orders: Orders placed directly with Tailors.lk through our platform
  • Sub-Brand Orders: Orders under Tailors.lk sub-brands or special collections
  • Production Control: Orders where Tailors.lk controls production, payment, delivery, or quality assurance
  • Full Management: Tailors.lk manages timelines, communication, and resolution processes
  • Refund Responsibility: Refunds and disputes handled directly by Tailors.lk unless otherwise stated

Marketplace Orders (Tailor Responsibility)

  • Direct Tailor Orders: Orders placed directly with listed tailors through their profiles
  • Tailor Control: Tailors control pricing, production, delivery, and customer service
  • Platform Facilitation: Tailors.lk facilitates discovery and communication only
  • Non-Execution: Tailors.lk does not execute the tailoring service
  • Primary Responsibility: Service responsibility lies entirely with the tailor or brand

Tender & Buyout Campaign Orders

  • Commercial Arrangements: Monthly tenders, category buyouts, or campaign allocations
  • Separate Agreements: Governed by separate commercial agreements and terms
  • Refund Exclusion: Typically excluded from standard refund policies
  • Platform Rights: Tailors.lk retains pricing and quality assurance rights
  • Campaign Specific: Terms vary by specific campaign or tender agreement

Tailor Responsibility Policy

All tailors on our platform accept specific responsibilities when they list their services or accept orders.

  • Order Acceptance Responsibility: Accepting an order equals accepting full responsibility for its completion
  • Universal Application: Applies regardless of order source (client, Tailors.lk, campaign, or referral)
  • Quality Delivery: Tailors must deliver agreed quality, timelines, and professional communication
  • No Abandonment: Work cannot be abandoned once accepted without proper cancellation procedures
  • Platform Suspension: Tailors.lk may suspend listings for breaches of these responsibilities
  • Professional Standards: Maintain professional conduct and timely communication throughout

Important: Tailors who repeatedly fail to meet these responsibilities may face permanent removal from the platform and loss of access to future opportunities.

Measurement & Custom Work Policy

Custom tailoring requires accurate measurements and clear communication. This policy outlines responsibilities for custom work.

  • Customer Responsibility: Customers must provide accurate, current measurements
  • Custom Nature: Custom garments are made specifically for individual clients
  • Fabric Irreversibility: Fabric cutting is irreversible once completed
  • Fit Adjustments: Post-production adjustments depend on individual agreements
  • Measurement Errors: Errors in provided measurements void refund eligibility unless platform-managed
  • Professional Assistance: Recommended to seek professional measurement assistance
  • Amendment Limitations: Significant measurement changes may require new garments

Measurement Tip: For best results, have measurements taken by a professional or use our recommended measurement guides. Double-check all measurements before submission.

Payment Policy

Clear payment policies ensure smooth transactions for all platform users.

  • Payment Handling: Payment typically handled by tailors unless platform-managed
  • Deposit Requirements: Deposits may be required for custom or large orders
  • Full Payment: Complete payment generally required before final delivery
  • Commercial Campaigns: May use subscription models, commissions, or other structures
  • Future Tools: Tailors.lk may introduce integrated payment tools in the future
  • Payment Disputes: Payment disputes follow the appropriate order classification
  • Currency: All transactions in Sri Lankan Rupees unless otherwise specified

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation terms vary depending on the production stage. Understanding these stages helps set clear expectations.

Before Production Begins

  • Full Refund: Typically eligible for full refund minus administrative fees
  • Timing: Must be requested before any materials are ordered or cut
  • Process: Formal cancellation request required

After Fabric Cutting

  • Partial Refund: May receive partial refund minus material costs
  • Material Costs: Fabric and material costs are non-refundable
  • Labor Charges: May include labor charges for work completed

During Stitching

  • Limited Refund: Limited to materials not yet used
  • Labor Costs: All labor costs up to cancellation point are charged
  • Completion Option: Option to complete garment with balance payment

After Completion

  • Non-Refundable: Generally non-refundable unless defective
  • Alteration Option: Alterations may be offered instead of refund
  • Quality Issues: Follows quality dispute process if defective

No-Shows for Fittings

  • Rescheduling: Multiple no-shows may incur rescheduling fees
  • Cancellation: Repeated no-shows may be treated as cancellation
  • Communication: Always communicate schedule changes in advance

Refund & Return Policy

Our refund policy balances customer protection with the realities of custom tailoring work.

  • Custom Garments: Custom-made garments are generally non-returnable except for defects
  • Inspection Period: 48-hour inspection period after delivery for platform-managed orders
  • Alteration Priority: Alterations offered instead of refunds where possible
  • Platform-Managed Orders: Follow Tailors.lk resolution procedures
  • Digital Services: Digital services and consultations are non-refundable
  • Defective Work: Quality defects follow our quality dispute resolution process
  • Standard Returns: Ready-made items may have different return policies

Refund Processing: Approved refunds for platform-managed orders are processed within 7-14 business days. Marketplace order refunds depend on individual tailor policies.

Delivery & Pickup Policy

Clear delivery policies ensure smooth handover of completed garments.

  • Pickup vs Courier: Options for in-store pickup or courier delivery
  • Risk Transfer: Risk transfers to customer after dispatch or pickup
  • Delivery Delays: Not responsible for delays from third-party logistics
  • Storage Timeline: Completed items stored for 30 days maximum
  • Abandoned Items: Unclaimed items may be donated or disposed after storage period
  • Delivery Charges: Delivery charges vary by location and service
  • Tracking: Courier deliveries include tracking where available

Alterations Policy

Alterations ensure proper fit and customer satisfaction with completed garments.

  • Qualifying Alterations: Minor adjustments for fit issues post-delivery
  • Timeframe: Alteration requests must be made within 7 days of delivery
  • Extra Costs: Complex alterations may incur additional costs
  • Free Rework Limits: Free alterations limited to minor adjustments
  • Material Limitations: Alterations limited by available fabric
  • Timeline: Alterations typically completed within 3-7 business days
  • Communication: Clear communication about alteration scope and timeline

Quality & Verification Policy

We maintain high standards to ensure customer satisfaction and platform integrity.

  • Quality Standards: All tailors must meet Tailors.lk quality standards
  • Random Inspections: Platform-managed orders subject to random quality checks
  • Verification Levels: Multiple verification levels for platform tailors
  • Featured Requirements: Featured tailors must maintain higher standards
  • Suspension Rights: Tailors.lk may suspend listings for quality breaches
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular quality assessment and feedback systems
  • Customer Feedback: Customer reviews influence quality ratings

Reviews & Content Policy

Authentic reviews help maintain trust and improve services for all users.

  • Authentic Reviews: No fake, purchased, or incentivized reviews
  • No Abuse: No abusive, threatening, or harassing content
  • Spam Prevention: No spam or irrelevant content in reviews
  • Moderation Rights: Tailors.lk moderates all reviews before publication
  • Removal Authority: We may remove reviews violating policies
  • Response Rights: Tailors may respond professionally to reviews
  • Content Ownership: Reviews become Tailors.lk property

Platform Usage Policy

Proper platform usage ensures security and integrity for all users.

  • No Scraping: No automated data collection or scraping
  • No Impersonation: No impersonation of other users or businesses
  • No Bypassing: No attempts to bypass platform for direct deals
  • No Spam: No spam or unsolicited communication
  • Data Protection: No misuse of platform data or user information
  • Account Security: Users responsible for account security
  • Legal Compliance: All usage must comply with applicable laws

Dispute Resolution Policy

A structured approach to resolving issues fairly and efficiently.

Internal Resolution Steps

  • Direct Communication: Parties attempt direct resolution first
  • Platform Mediation: Tailors.lk mediates if direct resolution fails
  • Evidence Submission: Both parties submit relevant evidence
  • Platform Decision: Tailors.lk makes final decision for platform-managed orders

Communication Period

  • Response Time: 48-hour response time for dispute communications
  • Documentation: Keep all communication and documentation
  • Professional Conduct: Maintain professional communication throughout

Escalation & Jurisdiction

  • Legal Escalation: Unresolved disputes may escalate to legal channels
  • Sri Lankan Jurisdiction: All disputes subject to Sri Lankan jurisdiction
  • Court Venue: Colombo courts as primary venue for legal proceedings

Data, Privacy & Cookies

We protect user data and maintain transparency about our data practices.

  • Data Collection: Data collected for platform functionality and improvement
  • Privacy Protection: User privacy protected according to our policies
  • Cookie Usage: Cookies used to enhance user experience and analytics
  • Policy Compliance: All data handling complies with our published policies
