Privacy Policy – Tailors.lk

This Privacy Policy explains how Tailors.lk collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and business data of visitors, customers, tailors, partners, franchisees, learners, and applicants in Sri Lanka. Tailors.lk respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) principles. This policy applies to all users of our platform and by using Tailors.lk, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Tailors.lk collects various types of information to provide and improve our services for the Sri Lankan tailoring community. The information we collect includes:

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location and address
  • Business details (for tailor listings)
  • Uploaded files (CVs, images, documents)
  • Measurements (if applicable for future custom tailoring services)

Account & Application Data

When you create an account or apply for opportunities with Tailors.lk:

  • Registration information
  • Career applications
  • Partner submissions
  • Tailor listing applications
  • Profile information and preferences

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our platform:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Search queries and browsing behavior
  • Interaction patterns and preferences
  • Referring website and exit pages

Communication Data

Information collected through direct communications:

  • Contact form submissions
  • WhatsApp click interactions
  • Email correspondence
  • Support tickets and inquiries

2. How We Collect Information

Tailors.lk collects information through various methods to ensure our platform functions effectively and provides the best experience for users across Sri Lanka:

  • Website Forms: Through registration forms, contact forms, and application submissions
  • Account Registration: When creating user accounts or tailor listings
  • Search Usage: When using our platform to find tailors in Colombo, Kandy, Galle, or other Sri Lankan cities
  • Cookies and Tracking Tools: Using cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analytics
  • Direct Communications: Through email, WhatsApp, and other direct contact methods
  • Third-Party Integrations: Through embedded services and external tools integrated with our platform

3. Why We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for several important purposes to ensure our platform functions properly and provides value to the Sri Lankan tailoring community:

  • Platform Operation: To operate and maintain our platform services
  • Tailor Verification: To verify tailor credentials and ensure quality service
  • Order Processing: To facilitate orders and transactions (when implemented)
  • Communication: To communicate with you about services and updates
  • Marketing and Updates: To send relevant information about our services
  • Analytics and Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Tailors.lk uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and improve our platform. Cookies help us:

  • Enable basic website functionality and navigation
  • Maintain your session and preferences
  • Understand how visitors interact with our platform
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized recommendations and services

For detailed information about our cookie usage, please see our Cookies Policy.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Tailors.lk is committed to protecting your personal information. We share your data only in specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in our operations
  • Hosting and Analytics Partners: With companies that provide hosting, analytics, and technical services
  • Payment Processors: With payment processing services (when implemented)
  • Franchisees and Partners: When required for legitimate business purposes
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

Important: Tailors.lk does not sell personal data to third parties. Any sharing is done strictly for operational, legal, or security purposes.

6. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Secure Servers: Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard security measures
  • Limited Access: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect data
  • Organizational Measures: Staff training and confidentiality agreements

Note: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but work continuously to improve our protective measures.

7. User Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data under applicable privacy laws:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent
  • Opt Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Operational Requirements: Data is retained as long as needed for platform operation
  • Legal Obligations: We may retain data longer to comply with legal requirements
  • Safe Disposal: When data is no longer needed, we securely dispose of it

The specific retention periods vary depending on the type of information and the purpose for which it was collected.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites that are not operated by Tailors.lk. These include:

  • Partner websites and services
  • Social media platforms
  • Payment processing services
  • External resources and tools

We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or security of these third-party websites. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

10. Children's Privacy

Tailors.lk services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate steps to remove such information.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or services. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.

We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. Continued use of Tailors.lk after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes will be communicated through our platform or via email to registered users when appropriate.
